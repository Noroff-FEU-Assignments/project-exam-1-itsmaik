YES, IT CAN BE FASHION

Fashion is a form of self-expression and creativity that transcends traditional boundaries. It is an art that is not confined to the runways or glossy magazine pages but is an everyday phenomenon that can be seen on the streets, in the office, or even at home. The concept that "everything can be fashion" stems from the idea that there are no fixed rules or limitations to what fashion can be.
